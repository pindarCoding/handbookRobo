# Self-assessment

## Question 1

**What collaboration style is most comfortable for Baby Boomers?**

a) Hierarchical structures and face-to-face meetings  
b) Digital-first and remote-only communication  
c) Informal chat-based collaboration  
d) Complete independence without teamwork  

✅ **Answer:** Hierarchical structures and face-to-face meetings  
Members of the Baby Boomer generation are familiar with conventional frameworks and prioritize straightforward, personal interactions. Although they have adjusted to using digital tools, they still prefer in-person, organized teamwork.

---

## Question 2

**Which approach helps to bridge generational gaps in collaboration?**

a) Ignoring generational differences  
b) Relying only on rigid hierarchies  
c) Discouraging hybrid work  
d) Reverse mentoring and open dialogue  

✅ **Answer:** Reverse mentoring and open dialogue  
Reverse mentoring enables younger staff members to impart their digital expertise while gaining insights from the experiences of their senior co-workers. When paired with open communication, this cultivates mutual respect and enhances collaboration across different age groups.
