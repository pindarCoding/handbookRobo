# Self-assessment

## Question 1

**Which of the following values is most often associated with Generation X?**

a) Respect for authority  
b) Autonomy and work-life balance  
c) Rapid feedback and inclusion  
d) Loyalty to one employer  

✅ **Answer:** Autonomy and work-life balance  
Generation X experienced a time of economic instability and changes within corporations. They typically prioritize independence, adaptability, and equity, placing a high importance on autonomy and balance in their lives.

## Question 2

**Which value is consistently important across all generations?**

a) Exclusivity  
b) High salaries  
c) Respect and collaboration  
d) Authority and hierarchy  

✅ **Answer:** Respect and collaboration  
Research indicates that, regardless of varying emphases, all generations place a high value on respect, collaboration, and a nurturing environment. These mutual values form a foundation for cooperation in the workplace.
