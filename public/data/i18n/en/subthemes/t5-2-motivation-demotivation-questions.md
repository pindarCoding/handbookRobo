# Self-assessment

## Question 1

**Which factor is a strong motivator for Millennials?**

a) Job security and loyalty to one employer  
b) Purpose and opportunities for growth  
c) Strict hierarchies and formal processes  
d) Avoiding digital tools  

✅ **Answer:** Purpose and opportunities for growth  
Millennials are driven by work that has significance, chances for advancement, and compatibility with their personal beliefs. Although stability matters, they perform best in settings that offer growth and a sense of purpose.

## Question 2

**What is a common demotivator for Generation Z?**

a) Too much feedback  
b) Lack of flexibility and poor communication  
c) Too many hybrid work options  
d) Mentoring opportunities  

✅ **Answer:** Lack of flexibility and poor communication  
Generation Z, being born into the digital age, anticipates adaptability and clear communication. They lose motivation when these elements are lacking or when systems are overly strict and unclear.
