# Reflection Questions

## Question 1

**Which of the following best represents an effective strategy for enhancing digital access in the workplace?**

a) Encouraging employees to use their personal devices and internet connections for work.  
b) Requiring employees to pay for their own digital tools to promote responsibility.  
c) Providing standardized devices and subsidizing internet access to ensure equal participation.  
d) Allowing only senior employees access to premium digital tools to increase efficiency.  

✅ **Answer:** Providing standardized devices and subsidizing internet access to ensure equal participation.  
Enhancing digital access means ensuring that all employees have reliable devices and connectivity. Providing standardized equipment and internet subsidies reduces socio-economic disparities and ensures consistent participation, productivity, and inclusion. Assuming employees’ personal resources are sufficient often perpetuates inequality.

## Question 2

**What is a key characteristic of an inclusive digital culture within organizations?**

a) Employees avoid admitting difficulties with digital tools to maintain professionalism.  
b) Leaders model openness to learning and create psychological safety for employees to seek help.  
c) Mistakes in using digital tools are penalized to maintain high performance standards.  
d) Digital training is treated as a one-time event rather than a continuous process.  

✅ **Answer:** Leaders model openness to learning and create psychological safety for employees to seek help.  
An inclusive digital culture promotes psychological safety, where employees can openly seek help and learn without fear of judgment. When leaders model curiosity and acknowledge their own learning gaps, it normalizes continuous digital growth and fosters equity, collaboration, and innovation.
